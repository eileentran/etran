Define The Relationship

The time is in Providence, Rhode Island.

Define The Relationship is a space for you to meditate on your relationship with the Sun. The Sun gives you vitality and at the same time it lets you rest. Its energy has never failed you since you were born. But during the late 1990s, the Sun been struggling to keep up with increased damage from emissions. Naturally, it hasn't been at its best but it still tries to keep up to support you. As of lately, the sun has been going through a lot of phases. Have you checked in on them?

Inspiration from Olafur Eliasson's The Weather Project.

Have you had the talk with the Sun yet?
